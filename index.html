Frequently Asked Questions About Brat Text Generator

What is the "Brat" style? What's its origin?

The "Brat" style originated from British singer Charli XCX's album cover design of the same name, released in 2023. This style is characterized by bright neon green backgrounds, minimalist black text, and slight blur effects. It quickly became popular on social media and formed a unique cultural phenomenon. Our Brat Text Generator perfectly recreates this simple yet visually impactful aesthetic style.

What can I use the generated images for?

You can use the generated images for personal purposes, including social media posts, personal projects, or non-commercial creations. They're perfect for Instagram stories, Twitter covers, personal blog illustrations, or fun content to share with friends. For commercial use, please ensure you have the rights to use the relevant fonts and design elements.

Why does my text look blurry?

The blur effect is one of the core features of the Brat style, giving the image a unique aesthetic feel. You can adjust the blur intensity using the "Blur Effect" slider, ranging from 0 (no blur) to 10 (high blur). The classic Brat style typically uses a slight blur effect of about 1.5px, but you can freely adjust it according to your creative preference.

How can I create the best effect for long text?

For longer text, the Brat Generator system will automatically adjust the font size to ensure the text fits the canvas. For best results, we recommend: 1) Keep your text short and concise, typically 20-30 characters works best; 2) For longer text, consider reducing font size and increasing canvas dimensions; 3) Consider breaking long sentences into multiple lines using the Enter key; 4) Try different alignment options - sometimes left or right alignment works better than center for long text. Our brat font generator tool provides flexible options to optimize your text display.

Can I use non-English characters?

Yes, our tool fully supports Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and other language characters. However, please note that due to different width-to-height ratios of different language characters, you may need to adjust font size or canvas dimensions for optimal display. Some special symbols and emojis may also require additional adjustments to display correctly.

Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

Yes, Brat Text Generator is fully compatible with mobile devices. The interface automatically adapts to your screen size, and all features work normally on phones and tablets. On mobile devices, you can easily adjust all parameters, preview effects, and download images directly to your device gallery, making it perfect for creating and sharing on the go.

How can I create larger sized images?

In the "Background Settings" area, you can customize the width and height of the image through the "Canvas Size" option, supporting images up to 2000×2000 pixels. For high-resolution devices or printing needs, we recommend using dimensions of at least 1200×1200 pixels. Note that after increasing the canvas size, you may need to adjust the font size accordingly to maintain visual balance of the text on the larger canvas.

Which format is best for sharing on social media?

Different formats are recommended for different uses:
- PNG format: The most versatile choice, maintaining high quality and clarity, suitable for Instagram, Twitter, and most platforms.
- JPG format: Smaller file size, suitable for scenarios requiring faster network loading, such as messaging apps or website backgrounds.
- SVG format: Vector format, can be scaled without loss of quality, ideal for designers or print work requiring further editing. SVG may not be supported on some social platforms but is very useful for web design and professional image editing.

How can I add custom fonts or upload my own background?

The current version focuses on providing the authentic Brat style using a fixed font to ensure style consistency. If you need to use custom fonts or backgrounds, you can first generate a basic image with our tool, then further customize it in image editing software such as Photoshop, GIMP, or Canva. We are considering adding more customization options in future versions, including font selection and background image upload features.

Are the generated images stored on your server?

No. All image processing is done entirely locally in your browser. We do not store any images you generate or text content you input on our servers. When you click the download button, the image is saved directly to your device, and no content is uploaded to our servers at any point in the process, ensuring your creative content remains completely private.
